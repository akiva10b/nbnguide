A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Haklita at the Airport

Misrad Haklita – The Ministry of Immigrant Absorption: 

The spouse who is not a Returning Resident will receive their Teudat Oleh upon arrival at the airport, as well as the first installment of Sal Klita for them and for the children. The name and information of the Returning Resident spouse will be added to the Teudat Oleh at the local branch of Misrad Haklita, at which time you will arrange Sal Klita payments for your entire family.
