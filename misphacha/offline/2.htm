A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Hapnim at the Airport

 Ministry of Interior – Misrad Hapnim: 

If you are an Oleh Chadash or an Ezrach Oleh, your Teudat Zehut will be issued at the airport upon arrival. Please ensure to bring all original documents. If you are a Katin Chozer, previous A1 visa holder or a returning resident, please visit your local branch of Misrad Hapnim (the Ministry of Interior) in order to issue your updated Teudat Zehut. A list of Misrad Hapnim branches around the country can be found on our website www.nbn.org.il.
