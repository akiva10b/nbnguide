NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Haklita

Call Misrad Haklita in your area to schedule a meeting with a Klita counselor. You must visit Misrad Haklita in the city listed on your Teudat Zehut.

You will need:

  • Teudat Zehut booklet
  • Teudat Oleh of spouse
  • Israeli and American/Canadian passports, if applicable
  • Transcript of entries and exits from Misrad Hapnim in case your are making Aliyah from Israel or your status was not approved from abroad by the Aliyah center of The Jewish Agency for Israel
  • Bank account details
  • 1 passport picture

You will:

  • Be added to your spouse’s Teudat Oleh
  • Receive a Toshav Chozer certificate
  • Receive information about Ulpanim for your spouse and children, housing benefits, living allowances, and other benefits
  • Find out when your sal klita payments will be deposited into your bank account
