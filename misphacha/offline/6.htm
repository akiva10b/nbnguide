NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Bituach Leumi

Proceed to “Bituach Leumi“ (National Insurance Institute) in order re-establish your residency in Israel.  As a Toshav Chozer (returning resident), you should have arranged supplementary health coverage for the duration of your waiting period.

You will need:

  • Israeli ID card (Teudat Zehut)
  • Teudat Oleh (received at Misrad Haklita)
  • Toshav Chozer certificate

At Bituach Leumi you will have to fill out 3 forms:

  • Declaration of settlement in Israel – שאלון לקביעת תושבות
  • Application for opening a file at Bituach Leumi – דין וחשבון רב שנת
  • Request for health insurance “Kupat Cholim“- קביעת תושבות ובקשה לרישום לקופ“ח

It takes approximately three weeks for your residency to be approved by Bituach Leumi.  At that time, you will be able to register your children at the Kupat Cholim.
