Misrad Hapnim at the Aiport

Ministry of Interior – Misrad Hapnim: 

Visit your local branch of Misrad Hapnim to issue your Teudat Zehut (Israeli ID card).  Make sure to bring your Israeli passport and two passport-size photos.  If you are joining a chartered Nefesh B’Nefesh flight, we will assist you in the processing of your Teudat Zehut during the flight and upon arrival at Ben Gurion Airport.
