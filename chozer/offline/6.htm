NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Haklita

Misrad Haklita (Ministry of Immigrant Absorption): Call Misrad Haklita in your area to schedule a meeting with your Klita counselor. You must visit Misrad Haklita in the city listed on your Teudat Zehut.

You will need:

  • Teudat Zehut booklet
  • Foreign and Israeli passports
  • Authorization of reporting to the IDF office or an official exemption or deferral from service.
  • All original documents presented in your Aliyah application (Israeli and foreign passports SINCE THE AGE OF 14, proof of studying in Israel if applicable, proof of living abroad if no passport is available, and a copy of your parents’ passports)
  • Transcript of entries and exits from Misrad Hapnim in case your are making Aliyah from Israel or your status was not approved from abroad by the Aliyah center of The Jewish Agency for Israel. Note: You can obtain this transcript from Misrad Hapnim by phone, by dialing *3450.
  • Bank account details
  • 2 passport pictures

You will receive:

  • Teudat Zakaut
  • Information about Ulpanim, housing assistance, Student Authority and other benefits
  • Find out when your Sal Klita payments will be deposited into your bank account
