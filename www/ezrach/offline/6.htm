NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Customs

Customs Authority – “Meches“:  Visit the local Customs office to open a file.

You will need:

  • Israeli ID booklet – “Teudat Zehut“
  • Teudat Zakaut provided to you by Misrad Haklita
  • All original documents provided to your Shaliach
  • Authorization of reporting to the IDF office or an official exemption or deferral from service
  • All passports from the date that you were first issued an Israeli passport or were first registered as an Israeli citizen
  • Transcript of entries and exits to Israel from the date that you were registered as an Israeli citizen (please note that Misrad Hapnim only keeps records of your entries and exits for the last 7 years, so this may be used as a supplement to the passports listed above)
  • Proof of studying in Israel on a one-year educational program recognized by Misrad Haklita (if applicable)
  • Spouse’s passport and Teudat Zehut, if applicable

Remember that your final status will be clarified only after opening a file with the Customs Authority. We recommend not shipping a container from abroad before there is a final decision about your status.
