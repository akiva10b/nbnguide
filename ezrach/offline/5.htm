Letter of Clarification from the IDF

You will need to clarify your military status with the IDF to obtain either a deferral or exemption from service. To obtain such a letter, please visit the “LishkatHaGiyus” at one of the following addresses:

  • Tel Aviv – Machane Tel Hashomer, 03-738-8888
  • Jerusalem – Rashi 103, 02-500-7000
  • Haifa – 12 Rechov Omar Kayam, 04-860-0700
  • Beer Sheva- 22 Rechov Yad Vashem, 08-665-0060
  • Tiberias- Rechov Natzeret, 04-671-7506
