NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Haklita

Call Misrad Haklita in your area to schedule a meeting with your Klita counselor. You must visit Misrad Haklitain the city listed on your Teudat Zehut.

You will need:

  • Teudat Zehut booklet
  • Teudat Zakaut received at the airport
  • Bank account details

You will receive:

  • Information about Ulpanim, housing assistance, Student Authority and other benefits
  • A schedule of when your Sal Klita payments will be deposited into your bank account
