NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Customs Authority

Customs Authority – “Meches“:  Visit the local branch of the Customs Authority office to open a file.

  • What you need:
    • Teudat Zehut (Israeli ID card)
    • Old or New Teudat Oleh
    • Current passports
    • All original documents provided to your Shaliach (Old Teudat Oleh and all passports since you left Israel as a temporary resident)
    • “Tamzit Rishum Murchevet” from Misrad Hapnim
    • Transcript from Misrad Hapnim with your entries and exits
    • Signed declaration from the Beit Mishpat HaShalom that you did not use car benefits in the past
    • Your spouse’s passport and Teudat Zehut

Remember that your final status and will be clarified only after opening a file with the Customs Authority. We recommend that you DO NOT ship a container from abroad before there is a final decision about your status.
