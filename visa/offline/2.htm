NBN – Post Aliyah Guide

A guide for your first few weeks as a new Oleh

Misrad Hapnim

Misrad Hapnim – Ministry of Interior:  Approximately 3 business days after your arrival, please visit your local Misrad Hapnim in order to update your Teudat Zehut or to issue a new one. Your Teudat Zehut number will be the same one that you received as a Temporary Resident.  If you previously volunteered as a soldier in Machal, you may receive a new TeudatZehut number. Please make sure to ask for a “Tamzit Rishum Murchevet” transcript fromMisrad Hapnim. Please note that you might need to schedule an appointment in order to complete this step.

  • You will need:
    • 2 passport pictures for any candidate over age 16
    • Original birth certificates for all family members
    • Original copy of your Marriage/Divorce/Spouse’s death certificate with apostillecertification
    • Original Proof of Judaism letter
    • Valid passports for all family members
